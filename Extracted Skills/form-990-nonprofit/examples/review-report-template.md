# Review Report Template

Template for the Form 990 compliance review findings report. Use this structure when generating the review deliverable in the Review branch.

## Report Structure

### 1. Cover Section

```
FORM 990 COMPLIANCE REVIEW REPORT

Organization: [Legal Name]
EIN: [XX-XXXXXXX]
Tax Year: [YYYY]
Review Date: [MM/DD/YYYY]
Prepared By: [Reviewer Name / ADF Haiti]

CONFIDENTIAL: This report is prepared for internal use and does not constitute
an audit, legal opinion, or tax advice. Consult a qualified CPA or tax attorney
for professional guidance on any finding.
```

### 10. Disclaimer

```
DISCLAIMER

This compliance review was conducted based on information provided and publicly
available IRS instructions for Form 990. This review:

- Does NOT constitute an audit of financial statements
- Does NOT constitute legal or tax advice
- Does NOT guarantee compliance with all applicable federal and state requirements
- Does NOT substitute for review by a qualified CPA or tax attorney

The findings and recommendations herein are based on the reviewer's understanding
of IRS Form 990 requirements as of the review date. Tax law and IRS guidance are
subject to change. The organization should consult with its CPA and/or tax counsel
before making changes to its filing.

Organizations filing Form 990 are reminded that the completed return (excluding
Schedule B contributor information) is a public document available for inspection
under IRC Section 6104.
```

## Output Format Options

Deliver the review report in the user's preferred format:

- **Markdown (.md)**: default; optimized for Roam Research/Typora ingestion
- **Word (.docx)**: use ADF brand standards (Prussian Blue headings, Aptos font, Pale Blue Lily table banding)
- **Excel (.xlsx)**: findings table only, for tracking remediation

If the user does not specify, default to Markdown with an offer to convert to DOCX.
