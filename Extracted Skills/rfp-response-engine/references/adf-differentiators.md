# ADF Differentiators

## Core Win Themes

Weave these themes throughout every proposal. Each theme includes proposal-ready language.

### 1. Fully Haitian-Led, Locally Based

ADF is a Haitian-founded, Haitian-led organization with all 10-12 staff members from
Fond-des-Blancs and surrounding communities. The Board of Directors is entirely Haitian.
This is not a satellite office of an international organization; it is a local institution
with permanent presence.

**Proposal language**: "ADF's entire team lives and works in Fond-des-Blancs, providing
permanent local presence rather than fly-in/fly-out consulting engagements. This eliminates
mobilization delays and ensures continuity throughout the project lifecycle."

### 2. Technical Services Provider, Not a Charity

ADF operates a for-profit services division that funds its community development mission.
Clients engage ADF as a technical partner, not as an aid recipient. This creates
accountability structures identical to commercial consulting.

**Proposal language**: "ADF operates as a professional technical services provider,
delivering contracted outputs against defined specifications and timelines. Revenue from
client engagements funds ADF's community development programs, creating a sustainable
operational model independent of grant cycles."

### 3. Integrated Data Collection Infrastructure

ADF maintains a permanent technology stack deployed in southern Haiti:

| Equipment | Specification | Capability |
|---|---|---|
| Arrow Gold RTK GNSS | Sub-5cm horizontal accuracy | Cadastral-grade parcel surveys |
| DJI Phantom 4 RTK | 2-4cm GSD | Orthomosaic, DSM, volumetric analysis |
| Android tablets (10+) | Rugged, field-grade | Mobile data collection via Fulcrum |
| Fulcrum platform | Cloud-based | Real-time data sync, validation, dashboards |
| ArcGIS Online | Enterprise license | Spatial analysis, web mapping, geodatabases |
| Power BI | Professional license | Data visualization and client dashboards |

**Proposal language**: "ADF owns and maintains all survey equipment locally, eliminating
procurement lead times and import logistics. Field teams train continuously on this
equipment, ensuring operational readiness within days of contract award."

### 4. Bilingual Operations

All field operations conducted in Haitian Creole (the mother tongue of every community
in Haiti). Documentation produced in French, English, or Haitian Creole per client needs.
No translation layer between data collectors and respondents.

**Proposal language**: "Every ADF enumerator, supervisor, and analyst is a native Haitian
Creole speaker, eliminating the translation barrier that compromises data quality in
externally staffed surveys."

### 5. Community Access and Trust

ADF has operated in Fond-des-Blancs since 2015 and maintains relationships with local
government (mairie, CASEC, ASEC), schools, churches, health facilities, cooperatives,
and community organizations across the commune and surrounding areas.

**Proposal language**: "ADF's decade-long presence in Fond-des-Blancs and the Sud
department provides pre-existing community relationships that dramatically reduce the
sensitization and access negotiation phases that delay externally managed data collection."

### 6. EkiTek Academy: Certified Training Center

ADF operates EkiTek Academy, a digital skills training center in Fond-des-Blancs offering
certified instruction in GIS, drone operations, data collection, and digital literacy.
This provides a pipeline of trained field staff and demonstrates ADF's training delivery
capability.

**Proposal language**: "Through EkiTek Academy, ADF trains and certifies data collection
professionals locally, maintaining a readily deployable pool of qualified field staff
beyond the core team."

## Past Performance Summaries

### 1. HDI / USAID CSSP: Organizational Capacity Assessment

- **Client**: Haitian Development Institute (HDI), under USAID Civil Society Strengthening Program
- **Value**: $150,000
- **Period**: 2022-2023 (18 months)
- **Scope**: Conducted Organizational Capacity Assessments (OCA) for local civil society
  organizations across Haiti. Administered structured assessments across 7 organizational
  domains, facilitated self-assessment workshops, produced baseline and endline reports,
  developed capacity strengthening plans.
- **Key Results**: Assessed multiple organizations, identified priority capacity gaps,
  delivered actionable strengthening plans adopted by client organizations.
- **Relevance**: Demonstrates M&E, organizational assessment, and capacity building expertise.

### 2. Bezwen Lekol: School Needs Census

- **Client**: ADF internal (community development program)
- **Value**: Internal program
- **Period**: 2023
- **Scope**: Comprehensive census of all schools in Fond-des-Blancs commune. Collected
  data on enrollment, infrastructure, staffing, resources, and needs using Fulcrum-based
  digital survey instruments. GPS-mapped every school. Produced community-level education
  dashboard.
- **Key Results**: Mapped and assessed every school in the commune, created the first
  comprehensive education database for Fond-des-Blancs, informed community development
  planning.
- **Relevance**: Demonstrates census-scale data collection, GIS integration, and
  community-level assessment capability.

### 3. ACCESO: Seed to Market Parcel Mapping

- **Client**: ACCESO (agricultural social enterprise)
- **Value**: $51,000
- **Period**: 2023 (phased: 350, 450, 200 parcels)
- **Scope**: RTK GNSS mapping of 1,000 agricultural parcels in southern Haiti for
  ACCESO's Seed to Market program. Collected boundary polygons, land use attributes,
  and farmer data. Delivered geodatabase with attributed parcel layer.
- **Key Results**: 1,000 parcels mapped to sub-5cm accuracy, delivered within phased
  timeline, geodatabase integrated into ACCESO's program management system.
- **Relevance**: Demonstrates parcel-scale GIS data collection, RTK GNSS operations,
  and agricultural sector experience.

### 4. FECCANO: Cacao Producer Georeferencing

- **Client**: FECCANO (Federation of Cacao Cooperatives of the North)
- **Value**: $39,700
- **Period**: 2023
- **Scope**: Georeferencing of 4,000 cacao producers across northern Haiti. Deployed
  12 two-person teams (binomes) with GNSS equipment. Collected producer locations,
  plot boundaries, and attribute data. Implemented multi-tier QA process.
- **Key Results**: 4,000 producers georeferenced, QA-validated dataset delivered,
  met accuracy specifications.
- **Relevance**: Demonstrates large-scale field mobilization, GNSS operations, and
  agricultural supply chain mapping.

### 5. Welthungerhilfe: Drone Operations Training

- **Client**: Welthungerhilfe (WHH)
- **Value**: Training contract
- **Period**: 2023
- **Scope**: Designed and delivered comprehensive drone operations training program
  for WHH staff and partners. Six-phase curriculum: theory, regulations, flight
  planning, field operations, data processing, reporting. Hands-on practicals with
  ADF drone fleet.
- **Key Results**: Participants certified in drone operations, curriculum materials
  delivered for ongoing use, post-training technical support provided.
- **Relevance**: Demonstrates training delivery capability and drone operations expertise.
