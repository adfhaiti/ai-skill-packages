## adf.ht About Page

### Overview
ADF is a Haitian-led and managed nonprofit. We believe that the socio-economic transformation of Fond-des-Blancs is possible if we tap into the latent talents of Haitians. We are a multi-faceted organization that functions as a service provider, knowledge management hub, collaboration and idea exchange forum, partnerships facilitator, and solutions incubator. Our work focuses on strengthening civil society organizations that serve Fond-des-Blancs, Haiti's poorest communities. We work at the grassroots level with Haitians to identify the most pressing needs and provide appropriate solutions. We strive to be a nonprofit that provides Haitians with a voice and power to make decisions in their own communities so they can take control of their futures. We believe that Haitians need to be at the forefront of the decision-making process. We are committed to fostering sustainable development led by Haitians.

### What We Aim to Solve
A lack of self-determination power in the hands of Haitians: The Haitian government is unrepresentative and citizens don't participate. In addition to being unrepresentative, the government has a weak presence in rural parts of the country. The government's shortcomings, and the general poverty of the population, have contributed to NGOs rendering many of the services that are available. So much so that headlines like "Haiti: A Republic of NGOs?" are commonplace. The problem isn't NGOs rendering services though. The problem is that the real decision-making power for nearly all of these NGOs are in foreign hands. The result is that Haitians do not have decision-making power in many of the choices that impact their economic, cultural, and social development.

### What We Are Trying to Accomplish
1. The economic transformation of FdB into a globally attractive community where FdB natives can lead prosperous lives.
2. The increase of decision-making power in the hands of Haitians over choices that impact their economic, cultural, and social development.
3. Strengthen local organizations so they can accomplish more and be able to take on more responsibility.
4. Make it possible for all kids growing up in Fond-des-Blancs, now and in the future, to pursue and realize their full potential.

## ekitek.ht About Page

EkiTek, a portmanteau of Equitable and Technology, is a pioneering digital skills training center located in Fond-des-Blancs, Haiti. As Haiti's first and only Certiport Authorized Testing Center (CATC), we're on a mission to bridge the digital divide and unlock life-changing opportunities for Haitians.

Vision: "We envision a Haiti where people don't have to leave their communities to access world-class education and career opportunities. By bringing globally recognized tech certifications to rural Haiti, EkiTek aims to transform Fond-des-Blancs into a thriving hub of digital innovation and entrepreneurship."

## ekitek.ht Services Page

Title: "Internationally Recognized Certifications"

EkiTek empowers individuals and communities in Haiti through accessible and impactful digital skills training. As the nation's first and only Certiport Authorized Testing Center (CATC), we provide internationally recognized certifications that open doors to new career opportunities and economic growth.

Certification Programs: Microsoft Office Specialist (MOS)

Learning features: modern computer labs, experienced local instructors, small class sizes, flexible scheduling (morning, evening, weekends), structured exam preparation, one-on-one tutoring.

Community Impact: economic growth (small business development), community development (strengthening local economy, keeping families together), social impact (bridging digital divide, equitable access to technology).
