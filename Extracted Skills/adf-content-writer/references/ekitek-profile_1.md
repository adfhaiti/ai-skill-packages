# EkiTek Academy Profile

## Identity

- Full name: EkiTek Academy
- Name origin: portmanteau of "Equitable" and "Technology"
- Haitian Creole spelling: EkiTek (accent on the e: EkiTek)
- Relationship: EkiTek Academy is a program of ADF Haiti, Inc.
- Website: https://ekitek.ht/
- Email: info@ekitek.ht
- Location: Fond-des-Blancs, Sud Department, Haiti

## Distinction

EkiTek is Haiti's first and only:
- Certiport Authorized Testing Center (CATC)
- ICDL Accredited Testing Center

No other institution in Haiti holds either accreditation. This is a genuine, verifiable first.

## Mission

Bridge the digital divide and unlock life-changing opportunities for Haitians through internationally recognized digital skills certifications.

## Vision

A Haiti where people don't have to leave their communities to access world-class education and career opportunities. Transform Fond-des-Blancs into a hub of digital innovation and entrepreneurship.

## Program Structure

- Format: 4-month cohorts
- Tuition: 20,000 HTG per cohort
- Schedule: morning, evening, and weekend options
- Class size: small, for personalized attention (~30 students per cohort at launch in 2023)
- Facilities: modern computer lab (located at L'Exode Secondary School campus, ADF headquarters)
- Exam preparation: structured prep, practice tests, one-on-one tutoring available
- Open enrollment: initially served local organizations, now open to the public

## Revenue

Training revenue contributes approximately 2.39M HTG annually to ADF's earned revenue.

## Community Impact Framing

When writing about EkiTek's impact, frame it around three pillars:
1. **Economic growth:** supporting small business development through digital skills
2. **Community development:** strengthening the local economy, keeping families together (people don't have to migrate for opportunity)
3. **Social impact:** bridging the digital divide, ensuring equitable access to technology in rural Haiti
