RE: Baseline Household Survey — Scope of Work and Budget, Contract 2025-01
Executive Summary

ADF will conduct a baseline household survey covering 1,400 households across 34 communities in the Sud department. The scope includes mobile data collection, GIS drone mapping of all target zones, and delivery of a cleaned, analysis-ready dataset within 60 days of contract execution.

All fieldwork is led by ADF's permanent staff. No subcontractors are engaged for data collection activities. ADF's three-pass QC protocol ensures data completeness above 95% before any dataset is delivered to the client.

ADF delivers data, not recommendations. All analytical conclusions in final reports reflect partner-defined indicators and ADF's independently validated methodology.

Scope of Work

ADF will design survey instruments, train 12 enumerators, and deploy mobile data collection using KoboToolbox. Drone mapping flights will cover all 34 communities, producing georeferenced orthomosaic outputs at 5cm/pixel resolution. Data cleaning and validation will follow ADF's standard protocol.
