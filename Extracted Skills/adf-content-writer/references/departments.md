## HCDF (Haiti Christian Development Fund)

An affiliated organization founded by Jean Thomas (ADF Board Secretary; Josiah Thomas's father). HCDF is a separate entity, not a program of ADF, but the two organizations collaborate closely.

**Relationship to ADF:** Affiliated organization; Jean Thomas serves on ADF's Board. ADF provides GIS and data services to HCDF. HCDF's programs frequently appear in ADF content as partnership examples.

**Key program: Jubilee Farms**
- Agricultural co-operative of 48 member landowners
- 58+ acres of land, 73 parcels mapped by ADF using Arrow Gold GNSS
- Goal: deploy large-scale mechanized agriculture across all parcels
- Replacing physical boundary markers (fences, stones) with digital GIS boundaries
- Results: roughly 4x more food production with mechanized methods vs. manual
- Farmers retain land ownership; co-op handles mechanized operations
- Profit allocation based on verified parcel size (transparent, data-backed)

**"Legacy in Agriculture" narrative:**
- "Securing the Harvest" framing: "occupied land" becomes "family legacy"
- GIS mapping gives farmers "peace of mind" about land ownership
- Enables investment in long-term improvements (farmers won't invest if tenure is insecure)
- Intergenerational benefit: documented boundaries protect future generations

**When writing about HCDF/Jubilee Farms:**
- Clarify that HCDF is a separate organization (not an ADF program)
- Frame as a partnership/collaboration: "ADF provides GIS services to HCDF"
- Use the Eos Positioning Systems case study as the source for specific claims
- Josiah's quote: "Together, we are producing crops that can be consumed locally or sold internationally. This is a great model to help our communities farm in ways that are both more sustainable and more ethical."
