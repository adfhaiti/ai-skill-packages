# ADF Haiti: Professional Service Descriptions

## Overview

ADF sells professional data collection, GIS/drone mapping, and program evaluation services to NGOs, bilateral donors, local government, and businesses. This earned revenue funds ADF's community development mission. ADF is not a charity doing surveys on the side; it is a professional services organization that happens to be a nonprofit.

## Data Collection Services

### Household and Community Surveys
- Census-scale household data collection (up to 18,500 households per engagement)
- Needs assessments, baseline studies, endline surveys
- Tool: Fulcrum (bilingual Haitian Creole/English forms with conditional logic, calculated fields, data events)
- Trained enumerator teams (scalable: 10-30 enumerators + supervisors per engagement)
- Quality control: GPS-verified locations, built-in validation, supervisor review workflows

### Program Monitoring and Evaluation
- Design and implementation of M&E frameworks
- Data collection for program indicators
- Analysis and reporting (Power BI dashboards, Excel workbooks)
- Baseline, midline, and endline surveys

### Organizational Capacity Assessments
- Structured assessments of civil society organizations
- Digital readiness evaluations
- Governance and management reviews

## GIS and Drone Mapping Services

### Drone Imagery
- Equipment: DJI Phantom 4 Pro v2.0
- Processing: DroneDeploy, Pix4D
- Outputs: orthomosaics at 2-4 cm resolution, digital surface models, digital terrain models
- Flight planning and basemap data capture

### Field GIS Data Collection
- Equipment: Eos Arrow Gold RTK GNSS receiver with ATLAS PPP corrections
- Software: ArcGIS Pro, Esri Field Maps, Fulcrum
- Coordinate system: WGS 84 / UTM Zone 18N (EPSG:32618)
- Data types: points, lines, polygons (parcel boundaries, infrastructure, agricultural plots)

### Data Products
- Parcel surveys and boundary mapping
- Infrastructure mapping (schools, water points, roads)
- Agricultural land use classification
- Vegetation analysis

## Digital Skills Training (via EkiTek Academy)

- Certifications: IC3, MOS Word, MOS Excel, QuickBooks, ICDL
- Haiti's only ICDL Accredited and Certiport Authorized Testing Center
- 4-month cohorts, 20,000 HTG tuition
- Modern computer lab with experienced local instructors

## Pricing Context

- Typical project range: $5K to $150K
- Duration: 1 to 18 months
- Per-household rate (census work): approximately $4.25/household (Bracing Neighbors reference)
- ADF does not price-compete with unregistered data collection firms on commodity survey work
- Minimum engagement: generally $5K+ (sub-$5K contracts require clear path to larger relationship)
